👑 About The Crippling Truth

Unfiltered. Unapologetic. Unstoppable. Meet the minds behind the satire.

🌟 Our Mission

The Crippling Truth aims to expose the absurdities of how society treats disabled people—one satirical headline at a time.

We blend biting satire, unapologetic humor, and sharp political commentary to highlight daily realities, including government failings, inaccessible housing, and patronizing inspiration porn.

  • ✔️ Laughter as a weapon against injustice.
  • ✔️ Humor breaking barriers & challenging misconceptions.
  • ✔️ Satire to hold those in power accountable.

If you’re easily offended, you might want to leave. If you’re ready for raw honesty and dark humor—welcome. You’re one of us.

👑 Meet Dame Penny Less – Editor-in-Chief

“They said being disabled was a full-time job. I just want the paycheck.” — Dame Penny Less

Dame Penny Less wasn’t always the queen of crippling satire. After becoming disabled and encountering the UK welfare system’s incompetence, she swapped caviar for care assessments and galas for government forms.

The Department of Witchcraft and Percussion (DWP) became her nemesis; society's ableist nonsense became her muse.

Her satirical commentary? Not just comedy—activism with a punchline.

📝 Dame Penny Less’s Philosophy:

  • ⚡ “Inspiration” is for Pinterest boards, not disabled people living their lives.
  • ⚡ Government policies should come with a joke warning—except they’re deadly serious.
  • ⚡ If we didn’t laugh at the system, we’d be screaming into the void.

📚 Penny's Upcoming Book:

_From Posh to PIP: A Cripple’s Tale_

A hilariously brutal memoir charting Penny’s fall from high society to high-stakes battles with benefit assessments. Coming soon—if she ever finishes arguing with the council.

“Society loves a disabled person they can pity or put on a pedestal. But a disabled person who talks back? Terrifying. So here I am. Terrifying—and telling the truth.” — Dame Penny Less
